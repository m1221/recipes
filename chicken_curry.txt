#READ ENTIRE RECIPE BEFORE STARTING IT

INGREDIENTS
- 2lb chicken thigh, chicken breast, or chicken; cut into small chunks
- 8 medium sized tomatoes; diced
- 1/2 large white onion; diced
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 6 tbsp chili powder
- 2 tbsp cayenne pepper
- 2 tbsp garam masala powder
- 2 tbsp madras curry powder
- 1 tsp cardamon powder
- 2 tbsp minced garlic
- 2 cups water

COOKWARE
-Large pan, 4-5 inches deep

INSTRUCTIONS
0. Prepare all ingredients.
1. Heat 1 cup vegetable oil in large pan until it is hot.
2. Add diced onions to pan, saute on medium-high heat until they are brown.
3. Add diced tomatoes, all powders, garlic, and 1 cup of water.
4. Cover pan with lid, cook on medium-ish heat, and stir every 3-5 minutes
until mixture has become saucy. ~25minutes. Turn down heat and add second
cup of water if mixture becomes too dry.
5. Add chicken and stir. Cover pan with lid and cook for an additional ~25 minutes.
6. Turn off heat.
7. Let sit on stove for ~20 minutes.
8. Salt to your liking.
